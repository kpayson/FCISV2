NCI Surgery Branch (SB) Modular Trailer 2 10B

The NIH NCI Surgery Branch (SB) Modular Trailer 10B located within the NIH northwest campus area adjacent to Building 10, is a Mobile Cleanroom. The facility complies with current Good Manufacturing Practices (cGMP). The space is approximately 400 gross square feet.

This trailer contains three (3) physically separated ISO Classified rooms; Entry Room, Anteroom and Cleanroom. The Entry Room meets the ISO Classification consistent with ISO 8. The Anteroom and Cleanroom meet ISO Class 7. The Trailer is being operated as a positive pressure cascading contamination control suite.

The mechanical systems include single 100% OA air handling unit and terminal units that supply, and control the air changes, its temperature, humidity and differential pressure. All supply air is provided by ceiling mounted fan filter HEPA modules. Cooling and heating are provided from independent chiller and electrical based heaters. All supply air to all the rooms is HEPA filtered while exhaust in clean room is also HEPA filtered before leaving the rooms via a low wall mounted HEPA filter housing.
